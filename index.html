Foundation Year

Get ready for university and gain direct entry to a wide range of undergraduate degrees. 
The International Foundation Diploma (IFD) is a university preparation programme designed to bridge the gap for international students between high school and undergraduate degree studies in the UK, US, Ireland or Australia. 

If you have completed your secondary school education in your own country and you are looking for a programme that will help you develop the English language skills and subject knowledge required for an undergraduate degree courses overseas, we have got you covered.

Participating in our International Foundation Programme will also increase your cultural awareness and prepare you for living and studying in a multi-cultural environment. There are a wide range of undergraduate degree courses you can progress to from the IFD in four main subject areas: Business, Finance, Computing or Engineering.
